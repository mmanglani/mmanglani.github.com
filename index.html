Miriam Manglani is an instructional designer and training manager with over 12 years experience and a Masters degree in Technology in Education from Harvard University with expertise in the field of eLearning, blended learning, and course production. When she's not working, Miriam enjoys swimming, reading, writing for her blog, working with clay on the pottery wheel, and above all, spending time with her husband, daughter, and twin boys.
